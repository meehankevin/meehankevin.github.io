My main hobbies are of a sporting nature, in particular Gaelic Football and Hurling. I play both for my local club St. Eunan's in Donegal.


I've won numerous accolades with St. Eunan's including a u16, minor, u21, junior and reserve football championships and minor and u21 hurling championships. With my county, I've won an Ulster U21 shield, All-Ireland 'C' championships at u16, minor and u21 level and at senior level I've won a Nicky Rackard Championship and an Allianz League Div 3A title. I also hold a Buncrana Cup runners-up medal with the county U16 footballers.


I also enjoy reading fictional novels, autobiographies and travelling. I spent three of my four college summers in the United States where I worked mostly in kitchens.
